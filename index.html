Unlighted - Demo

Will you be the ultimate test subject?

If you decide to undertake this long and hard trip with us you will discover a great secret hidden from humanity, so everything you see here is totally confidential. Therefore we ask for complete discretion, if you manage to get out, of course.

  • About The Game
  • Main Mechanics

Unlighted is a First Person Puzzle video game where you will have to overcome multiple tests and discover the eerie truth sorrounding the world. With the help of the light (and the abscence of it) you will discover the secrets around Umbral Alchemy, the group that is experimenting with your life.

Use the light and all the devices that Umbral Alchemy puts in your disposal to overcome all the tests and know the truth. Scan the Umbralita to know their location and light it up to objectify it.
